E-learning / Prof. Ulrik Schroeder

"Prime consists of a supported digital media learning and knowledge management system with corresponding applications for mobile devices. This should not only improve self-directed learning in the workplace and provide immediate assistance in the work process, but it should also be possible, present information, documentation, supplement guidelines and working documents and provide other employees in the integrated social network. The applications also enable the intelligent partitioning and storing complex documents in the smallest possible, reusable units, regardless of the format of the document. This allows relevant information optimally filtered and additionally in be provided a new context. "
